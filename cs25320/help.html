Introduction and Controls

The main goal of Aster-Risk is to survive and use your lasers in order to destroy the oncoming asteroids in order to score points. Througout the game, several types of power-ups will spawn which will help you blast your way through the debris!

CONTROLS

Move the mouse to move the ship. Clicking will result in the ship firing a laser. Arrows keys and spacebar may also be used for keyboard controls, but mouse controls are heavily preferred.
